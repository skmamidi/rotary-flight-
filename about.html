About Rotary Flight Mission

A science museum exhibit mixed with a game-based study guide.

What This App Teaches

Rotary Flight Mission helps students master rotary-wing aircraft concepts at a 5th grade reading level while preparing for Science Olympiad competitions. Topics include:

  • How helicopters, autogyros, and tiltrotors create lift
  • The four forces of flight in rotary-wing context
  • Rotor systems, torque, and anti-torque solutions
  • Flight controls: collective, cyclic, yaw, pitch, roll
  • History and pioneers of rotary flight
  • Real-world applications and future technology
  • Competition-style quizzes and mock tests

Who This App Is For

Primary Users

  • 5th grade students
  • Motivated upper-elementary learners
  • Science Olympiad participants
  • Parents and coaches helping students prepare

Secondary Users

  • Teachers using the app for mini-lessons
  • Teams using it for review practice
  • Homeschool families

How to Study With It

We recommend following the Study Path for the best results. Each topic page includes:

  1. Learn: Read the concept explanation.
  2. See: Explore diagrams and animations.
  3. Play: Try the interactive simulation or mini-game.
  4. Test: Answer the Quick Check questions.
  5. Review: Read the Olympiad Edge tips.

Badges & XP

Earn XP by completing pages, finishing interactives, and acing quizzes. Collect badges like Rotor Rookie, Lift Master, and Mock Test Ace. Climb the ranks from Cadet to State-Level Contender.
