Yaw, Pitch, Roll

The three ways an aircraft can rotate.

Core ⏱ 5 min ○ Not completed
🎯
Why this matters

These three terms describe every aircraft movement. Tests often ask which control produces which motion.

↔️

Yaw

Nose left or right. Controlled by tail rotor pedals.

↕️

Pitch

Nose up or down. Controlled by cyclic.

↩️

Roll

Tilting side to side. Controlled by cyclic.
