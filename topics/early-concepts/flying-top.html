Why this matters

The flying top showed that spinning blades could create upward force centuries before engines existed.

What the Flying Top Is

The Chinese bamboo dragonfly (around 400 BC) was a simple toy with feathers mounted on a stick. When spun rapidly between the hands, it lifted into the air.
