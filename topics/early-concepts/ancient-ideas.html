Why this matters

Understanding early inspiration shows that flight science began with careful observation of nature.

Observing Birds

Early humans watched birds soar and wondered how they stayed aloft. They noticed wings, feathers, and the importance of air.

Inspiration vs Engineering

Observation inspired ideas, but engineering was needed to make them real. Nature showed what was possible; inventors had to figure out how to build it.
