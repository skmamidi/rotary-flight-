Why this matters

De la Cierva invented the autogyro, proving that unpowered rotors could provide safe lift.

Who He Was

Juan de la Cierva (1895–1936) was a Spanish engineer who invented the autogyro. His C.1 first flew in 1923.

Contribution

He proved that a rotor could create lift without engine power, using autorotation. This influenced later helicopter designs.
