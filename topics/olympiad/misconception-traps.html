Misconception Traps

Don't fall for the traps that catch most students.

This page trains you on the most commonly missed ideas. Mastering these will put you ahead of the competition.

Trap 1: Autogyro = Helicopter?

Wrong statement: "An autogyro is just another type of helicopter."

Why it's wrong: Autogyros have unpowered rotors and cannot hover. Helicopters have powered rotors and can hover.

Trap 2: Tail Rotor Creates Main Lift?

Wrong statement: "The tail rotor creates most of the helicopter's lift."

Why it's wrong: The tail rotor only counters torque and controls yaw. The main rotor creates lift.

Trap 3: Collective = Cyclic?

Wrong statement: "Collective and cyclic do the same thing."

Why it's wrong: Collective changes total lift. Cyclic tilts the rotor disk for direction.

Trap 4: VTOL = Hover Only?

Wrong statement: "VTOL means the aircraft can only hover."

Why it's wrong: VTOL means vertical takeoff and landing. Many VTOL aircraft also fly forward fast.

Trap 5: Torque = Drag?

Wrong statement: "Torque is the same as drag."

Why it's wrong: Torque is a twisting reaction. Drag is air resistance opposing motion.
