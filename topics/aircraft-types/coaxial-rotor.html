Why this matters

Coaxial designs are compact and safe near obstacles because they have no tail rotor.

Counter-Rotating Stacked Rotors

Two rotors are mounted one above the other on the same mast. They spin in opposite directions, canceling torque. The Kamov Ka-50 is a famous example.
