Key Concepts

  • Rotary flight uses spinning blades for lift.
  • Fixed-wing aircraft need forward speed and runways.
  • Four forces: lift, weight, thrust, drag.
  • Helicopters can hover; autogyros cannot.
  • Tiltrotors combine VTOL and fast cruise.

Major Pioneers

  • Leonardo da Vinci — Aerial screw concept
  • Juan de la Cierva — Autogyro (1923)
  • Igor Sikorsky — VS-300 practical helicopter (1939)
  • Arthur M. Young — Stability at Bell
