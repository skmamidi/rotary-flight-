Rotor Blade Lift

How a spinning wing pulls the aircraft up.

Why this matters

One of the highest-priority pages. Understanding blade lift is the key to understanding hovering, climbing, and autorotation.

Rotor Blade as Airfoil

A rotor blade is shaped like a wing. As it spins, air moves faster over the curved top than the flat bottom. This pressure difference creates lift.

Why Spinning Matters

Speed increases lift. The faster the blade spins, the more air flows over it and the more lift is generated. That is why rotor RPM is critical.

🏆
Olympiad Edge

Lift comes from air movement and blade shape/pitch, not engine power alone. Engine power spins the rotor; aerodynamics create lift.
