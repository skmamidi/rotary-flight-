Why this matters

Da Vinci's sketch proved that humans were thinking about rotary lift long before engines existed.

Leonardo's Vision

Leonardo da Vinci sketched an "aerial screw" in the 1480s. It was a large helical screw made of linen and wire, meant to be spun by human muscle power.

Why It Didn't Fly

Human muscles cannot generate enough power to lift the screw and a person. The materials were also too heavy and flexible. But the concept was visionary.
