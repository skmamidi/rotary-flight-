Why this matters

Knowing the sequence of breakthroughs helps you answer history questions and understand how each invention built on the last.

Timeline of Rotary Flight

Ancient — Humans observe birds and insects, dreaming of flight.
400 BC — Chinese flying top (bamboo dragonfly) shows rotor lift concept.
1480s — Leonardo da Vinci sketches the aerial screw.
1923 — Juan de la Cierva's autogyro makes its first successful flight.
1939 — Sikorsky's VS-300 achieves first practical helicopter flight.
1942 — R-4 becomes the first mass-produced helicopter.
1946 — Bell Model 47 becomes first certified civilian helicopter.
1989 — V-22 Osprey tiltrotor proves hybrid vertical and forward flight.
2020s — Electric air taxis and urban air mobility prototypes tested.
🏆
Olympiad Edge

Knowing sequence strengthens concept understanding. Tests may ask: "What came first, the autogyro or the practical helicopter?" Answer: autogyro (1923) before VS-300 (1939).
