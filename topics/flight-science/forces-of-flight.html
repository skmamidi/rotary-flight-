Forces of Flight

The four forces that every pilot must balance.

Why this matters

Every flight maneuver is about balancing four forces. Understanding them is the foundation of all flight science questions.

⬆️

Lift

Upward force created by air moving over blades. Opposes weight.

⬇️

Weight

Downward pull of gravity on the aircraft. Opposes lift.

➡️

Thrust

Force that moves the aircraft. In helicopters, the rotor provides thrust by tilting. Opposes drag.

🌬️

Drag

Air resistance opposing motion. Parasitic drag and induced drag both matter. Opposes thrust.

Olympiad Edge

In a hover, thrust and drag are small or balanced in different directions, while lift must equal weight. In forward flight, the rotor tilts, so lift is no longer purely vertical and thrust becomes more significant.
