Why this matters

Helicopters are inherently unstable. Pilots must constantly make small corrections to maintain controlled flight.

Stability vs Control

Stability means the aircraft tends to return to steady flight after a disturbance. Control means the pilot can command changes. Helicopters trade stability for maneuverability.
