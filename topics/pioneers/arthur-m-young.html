Who He Was

Arthur M. Young (1905–1995) was an American inventor who worked with Bell Helicopter. He focused on stability and control systems.

Contributions

  • Developed stabilizer bar for helicopters
  • Improved pilot control response
  • Helped design early Bell helicopters
