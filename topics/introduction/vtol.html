Why this matters

VTOL is one of the biggest advantages of rotary flight. It lets aircraft operate from rooftops, forests, ships, and disaster zones where runways do not exist.

Define VTOL

VTOL stands for Vertical Takeoff and Landing. It means an aircraft can lift straight up into the air without rolling down a runway, and can land straight down again.

Helicopters are the most common VTOL aircraft. Some tiltrotors and specialized jets can also do VTOL.

Olympiad Edge

VTOL is an ability, not an aircraft type by itself. Do not say "a VTOL" as if it is a specific kind of plane. Instead, say "a helicopter with VTOL capability" or "a VTOL aircraft."
