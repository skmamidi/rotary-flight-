Why this matters

Tandem rotors cancel torque without a tail rotor, freeing up full engine power for lift.

Front and Rear Rotors

The front and rear rotors spin in opposite directions. Their torques cancel. This design is used for heavy cargo helicopters like the CH-47 Chinook.
